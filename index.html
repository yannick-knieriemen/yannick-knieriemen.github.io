Yannick Knieriemen

PhD Candidate in Economics · ifo Institute & LMU Munich

Research Interests: Climate Modeling (IAMs), Neural Networks, and Environmental Economics.

I am a doctoral researcher at the ifo Center for Energy, Climate, and Resources and LMU Munich. My research is supported by a scholarship from the German Federal Environmental Foundation (DBU) and focusses on climate modeling and inequality.

I am supervised by Prof. Karen Pittel (ifo Institute and LMU Munich) and Prof. Christian Traeger (University of Oslo).
