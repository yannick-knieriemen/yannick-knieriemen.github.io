Yannick Knieriemen

PhD Candidate in Economics · ifo Institute & LMU Munich

I am a doctoral researcher at the ifo Institute (Center for Energy, Climate, and Resources) and LMU Munich. My research is supported by a scholarship from the Deutsche Bundesstiftung Umwelt (DBU).

I hold a Master's degree in Economics from the University of Mannheim (Grade 1.2, with distinction). My academic journey included a research stay at the University of Oslo, and I am currently co-supervised by Prof. Christian Traeger.

Research

I am currently working on topics related to Climate Modeling, Integrated Assessment Models (IAMs), and the intersection of climate policy with global inequality. My work explores how the diffusion of clean technologies can be optimized within diverse economic frameworks.

Updates and working papers will be posted here as they develop.
