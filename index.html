Yannick Knieriemen

PhD Candidate in Economics · ifo Institute & LMU Munich

I am a doctoral researcher at the ifo Institute (Center for Energy, Climate, and Resources) and LMU Munich. My research is supported by a scholarship from the Deutsche Bundesstiftung Umwelt (DBU) and focuses on climate modeling and inequality.

I am supervised by Prof. Karen Pittel (ifo Institute and LMU Munich) and Prof. Christian Traeger (University of Oslo).
