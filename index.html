Welcome!

Yannick Knieriemen

PhD Candidate in Economics · ifo Institute & LMU Munich

I am a doctoral researcher at the ifo Center for Energy, Climate, and Resources and LMU Munich. My research is supported by a scholarship from the German Federal Environmental Foundation (DBU).

I am interested in how climate policy interacts with global inequality and the diffusion of clean technologies. My methodological focus lies at the intersection of Climate Modeling (IAMs) and Neural Networks.

I am supervised by Prof. Dr. Karen Pittel (ifo Institute and LMU Munich) and Prof. Dr. Christian Traeger (University of Oslo).
